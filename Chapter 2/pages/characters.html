Characters

Protagonist

VA: Xander Mobus

A second-year high school student who moved in the spring and transfered to Shujin Academy. One day, with his back against the wall, his powers as a Persona-user awakens. He lives as a normal student in the day as a tenant of a caf√© owned by a family friend. After school, he turns into a Phantom Thief who upheaves the city.

Kasumi Yoshizawa

VA: Laura Post

A beautiful girl who transfers to Shujin Academy the same spring as the protagonist. She's a talented gymnast since middle school and Shujin Academy holds high hopes for her because of her excellent athletic records.

Ryuji Sakamoto

VA: Max Mittelman

A fellow second-year student at Shujin Academy. Although his words and actions can be crude, he has a caring, dependable, and outgoing personality. Due to an accident, he is labeled as a delinquent and fellow students began distancing themselves from him. After befriending the recently transferred protagonist, he awakens his abilities as a Persona-user.

Morgana

VA: Cassandra Lee Morris

A mysterious cat-like creature that the protagonist and Ryuji encounter in the Metaverse, he decides to accompany them shortly after. He can communicate with humans and claims to be able to transform into various objects, yet many things about him remain shrouded in mystery. Although he can be aggressive at times, he's also incredibly selfless and supports his teammates. He often claims "I'm not a cat!"

Ann Takamaki

VA: Erika Harlacher

A fellow second-year student at Shujin Academy, and a classmate to the protagonist. She's an attractive girl who is one quarter American and models on the side. She's free-spirited and has an honest personality, but due to her striking appearance, she's isolated from everyone around her. After befriending the protagonist, an incident acts as a catalyst to awaken her abilities as a Persona-user.

Yusuke Kitagawa

VA: Matthew Mercer

A second-year student at Kosei High School who specializes in painting. His attractive appearance draws attention, but his unique artistic sensibilities often lead him out of touch with others. He's a live-in apprentice for Ichiryusei Madarame, a master artist. A turn of events leads him to befriend the protagonist and awaken his abilities as a Persona-user.

Makoto Nijima

VA: Cherami Leigh

A third-year attending Shujin Academy. She is intelligent, well-mannered, and serves as the student body president. Calm and collected, she has a strong sense of responsibility and is quick to take action, but due to her straight-forward personality she can be quite stubborn. A series of events triggers her powers as a Persona-user before she joins the Phantom Thieves. She acts as the brains of the team.

Futaba Sakura

VA: Erica Lindbeck

A girl one year younger than the portagonist. She boasts an incredibly mathematical brain along with a prodigious talent for computer programming, but struggles with social communication. She lives a cloistered life trapped in her own home. She encounters the protagonist one day and awakens her abilities as a Persona-user.

Haru Okumura

VA: Xanthe Huynh

A third-year student as Shujin Academy, and the daughter of the CEO of one of the largest food companies in the country. Her soft appearance and docile personality present someone capable of maintaining positive relationships, but due to the environment she grew up in, she struggles to find value in building meaningful relationships, often avoiding them altogether. What will lead to her awakening her abilities as a Persona-user...?

Goro Akechi

VA: Robbie Daymond

A third-year high school students who is also a professional detective. With a track record of solving numerous crimes, he maintains a respectable reputations even among law enforcement officials. His sharp intellect, social personality, and good looks attracts the attention of many. He frequently appears on media, often touted as the "The Second Coming of the Detective Prince." Although he is formally in the position of chasing after the Phantom Thieves, he eventually works alongside them.
